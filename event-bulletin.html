What’s up Motorballers? We’ve added a new section to the server for some new events we’d like to start organizing as a community. The tournaments have been a lot of fun as well as a positive motivator for many of us and we want to help build on that idea by putting together small community events for all who would like to participate. That said, our first event will be a win competition which will run throughout Season 10! The person who accumulates the most wins this season will receive a special server role and Season 10 bragging rights for all of eternity. • To enter the contest, you’ll need to post a screenshot of your in game stats page at the start of the season in the new channel #entry-＆-submissions. • At the end of Season 10, you will need to post a second screenshot displaying your final stat totals for the season. The total number of wins gained from start to finish will determine the event winner. • Submissions posted either before the season begins or after the season ends will not be counted. Please try to keep all event related questions and discussions in #event-discussion. Also, slow mode has been activated in #entry-＆-submissions so please limit your messages in this channel to screen shot submissions only. Good luck all! (edited)
